Who She Is

Born to Care, Built to Connect

Sixty-two years of living fully, loving fiercely, and showing up for the people who matter most. Liaura Matthews has been lighting up the Bay Area since day one — from the hallways of El Cerrito High School to the hospital floors where she found her calling as a Registered Nurse.

There's a warmth to Liaura that you feel the moment she walks into a room. Maybe it's the laugh that fills every corner, or the way she remembers the small things about everyone she meets. As a nurse, she's held the hands of people through their hardest days. As a friend, she's the one you call first — because she actually picks up, and she always knows what to say.

She's the friend who organizes the white party, who convinces you to try the new restaurant, who shows up with wine and the best stories. She loves live music, outdoor adventures, art galleries, and any excuse to gather the people she loves around a table.
